About Sienna

Basic info

  • Age = ?? (probably 16/17, possibly immortal)
  • Type = Black tabby
  • Lives with two other cats: Nolan and Newton
  • Gets tuna/salmon daily with her arthritis supplement
  • Demands water directly from the tap, but refuses to use a water fountain
  • Missing one front tooth, so her lip sometimes get caught in an "Elvis snarl"

Likes

  1. Being picked up, carried, cuddled
  2. Food - fish, dreamies, cat yoghurt, cat milk, cat soup
  3. Belly rubs
  4. Sneezing on people

Dislikes

  1. Playing with other cats
  2. Getting her claws trimmed
  3. Being brushed

Early Life

Sienna grew up in a hoarder's house with many other cats, scavenging for food, until she was rescued by the RSPCA. We adopted her from the RSPCA and she promptly hid under the oven. She slowly gained confidence until she became the snuggle-loving, tap-demanding cat we know and love today.

Daily Routine

Sienna's daily routine starts with excessively meowing at the first person up to prepare her tuna/salmon and supplement. Then, she will either lie on my bed or windowsill for some sunbathing for most of the morning. Occasionally she will catch someone at a sink and demand tap water, or sometimes bother someone to hold her while they try to do work.
